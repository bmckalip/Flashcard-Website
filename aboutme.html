Brian McKalip

Hi, I'm Brian. Originally from Chapel Hill, North Carolina, I've lived in the Tampa Bay region for over 15 years. I attended The University of South Florida from 2013 - 2017 and recently graduated with my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. I began programming my senior year of High School for an elective class where we created basic webpages, console applications in C, and programmed arduino microcontrollers. This class became the impitis for my decision to pursue computer science as my major at USF.

In my free time, during highschool and early into college, I worked as a volunteer server administrator for a (non-discontinued) Minecraft server community, where I began to write java plugins for the Minecraft multiplayer API Bukkit, aswell as managing configuration and backups in a Centos server environment remotely over SSH and acting as a community manager. I enjoy programming and all things technology, and am looking forward to beginning my career as a developer in today's growing technology field.
